How to use

  1. Open a job posting on LinkedIn, Glassdoor, Indeed, or any site.
  2. On a job site, click the logo (bottom-right) to review detected fields, then add to your board.
  3. On LinkedIn’s job list view, select a job first — detection follows the detail pane.
  4. If details are missing, highlight the job title and company, then click Add job.
  5. Use the side panel Board tab to drag jobs through your pipeline.
  6. Use All jobs to search, view full descriptions, and export your data.
